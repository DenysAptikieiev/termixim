About Us

TERMEXIM LTD is a company specializing on the Ukrainian agricultural products. The superb Ukrainian fertile soils, the bountiful harvests, the hard-working farmers – everything makes Ukraine a desirable object for agricultural investments. The path to EU chosen by Ukraine demands to ensure high quality standards

TERMEXIM LTD specializes in grain, oil-bearing crops, legumes and fertilizers. We buy these products directly from the producers, arrange shipping lots, organize storage and certification, prepare all the supporting documents, freight ocean vessels

We act in accordance with the European legislation and provide the product of the highest quality delivered in time

Services

TERMEXIM LTD struggles to satisfy all the needs of its customers by offering them the beneficial marketing solutions. We understand our customers’ needs and help them to optimize their supply chain management. That builds long-term trusted partnerships. Because we work with our customers, not against them, we provide them the full package of commodity supply, including origination, storage, certification, logistics, finance and risk management

We select our suppliers in Ukraine with the utmost care in order to provide our customers the commodities of the best quality. The commodities we sell have the most profitable cost – quality relation. TERMEXIM LTD provides the most profitable schemes to the farmers which encourage them to sell their best products. We attract farmers by both marketing and non-marketing services. Our grain brokers travel to various regions of Ukraine and personally select the best crops for our customers in the required quantities. The reputation of TERMEXIM LTD certifies that all our products match the specified class and grade. Our professionals successfully estimate land production possibilities, study crop rotation history, irrigation and fertilization facilities

One of our main concerns is storage organizing for the agricultural products sold for export. Because in most of cases a single shipload consists of hundreds small local loads, the proper storage is vital to ensure the products quality. The elevators and granaries in Ukraine provide services on accumulation, storage, fumigation, certification. We work with the reliable service providers only which are internationally certified for grain export. In future we plan building our own storage premises to ensure the highest quality standard of our crops at the attractive storage cost

TERMEXIM LTD organizes safe and effective transportation of our commodities. Our multimodal team chooses the best transport options by road, rail and sea. Because our volumes worldwide are large, the carriers offer us the best transportation rates. We carry agricultural commodities both in bulk and in containers depending on the customer’s needs. TERMEXIM LTD team is proud of its logistics experience and its commitment to deliver the goods safely and timely

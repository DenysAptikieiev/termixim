Commodities

Ukraine is the world’s greatest source of the numerous agricultural commodities: grain and legumes, milk and meat, oil and fertilizers, sugar and flour etc. Ukrainian fertile soils are among the best in the world. Ukraine now struggles to adjust its agricultural business to the international needs and to produce the products of the high quality. Meanwhile, Ukrainian grain, legumes, oil-bearing crops and fertilizers have already won the worldwide reputation

Legumes

Legumes (Fabaceae ) are used both as food grain seed and livestock forage and silage. Legumes is the only vegetable culture which has a valuable vegetable protein which can substitute an animal protein. Legumes are rich in vitamins and microelements such as folate, manganese, thiamin, magnesium, iron. That is why legumes are largely used in vegetarian diet. Ukrainian legumes are represented by beans, soybeans, peas, lentils, lupins etc. Ukrainian legumes market is yet to develop and increase, since Ukrainian cuisine does not consume much legumes

Oil crops

Ukraine is one of the leading world’s exports of oil-bearing crops and their products. They include sunflower seeds which are a symbol of Ukraine, rapeseed, linaceae, corn, cannabaceae etc. The vegetable oil is healthy and full of essential elements and vitamins. Oil-bearing crops contain a triglyceride which is extracted from a plant as an oil. Vegetable oils are used by humans for centuries for food and fuel. Vegetable oil adds to culinary dishes its rich flavor. Vegetable oils are widely used in cosmetics and pharmaceutical industries (soaps, candles, skin products, perfumes, ointments etc.) In mechanics vegetable oil is a part of lubricants. Vegetable oil is an irreplaceable ingredient of pet food. It is also is used a fuel since ancient times

Grain

Grain is the most important vegetable plant in human history. In ancient times good grain harvest used to mean prosperity and happiness, while bad harvest of grains symbolized poverty and death. Ukraine is famous worldwide for its grain. Ukraine’s fertile soils, favorable climate conditions and cheap grain costs lead this country to the leaders of the world grain market. It is certain, that Ukraine does not use its full grain potential, so the harvest figures could be doubled in future in case of the improved management. Ukrainian grain is represented by wheat, rye, barley, millet, oats, buckwheat and others. The main problem of grain production in Ukraine is grain storage. Building new granaries and new transshipment complexes are among our primary plans

Forwarding

To build a sophisticated logistics strategy means to improve products costs. Ukraine has a favorable geographic location being quite near the main consumer locations (Middle East and Northern Africa). Ukrainian fields are also located quite near its main maritime ports – Odessa and Chernomorsk (Odessa region). That allow to optimize transportation costs. TERMEXIM LTD has its own team of highly professional and motivated logistics specialists to arrange reliable, safe and timely delivery of various bulk and containerized commodities, i.e. grains, oil-bearing crops, fertilizers, legumes, sugar, flour etc. TERMEXIM LTD buys its own grain railcars to provide a smooth inland transportation. TERMEXIM LTD freights large ocean vessels to carry bulk products. Some commodities are transported in containers with and without packing based on the consignee’s needs. TERMEXIM LTD offers a multimodal transportation chain (maritime, railway, vehicular to satisfy any customer’s order. Our buyers are assured that their cargo is safe in one party’s hands from origination to destination

Sea

Sea transportation is the cheapest way to deliver agricultural commodities. Ukraine has several large maritime ports, among which there are Odessa, Chernomorsk (Odessa region) and Yuzhny. These ports are connected with all major regions my railways and highways and have developed infrastructure for agricultural needs

Road

Grain and oil-bearing crops are delivered to railways granary complexes throughout Ukraine by road transport. TERMEXIM LTD has its own specialized grain carries to ensure commodity safety. Our transport is certified for grain transportation. Road transport is quick and accessible. We can calculate delivery with accuracy up to a minute. Well planned logistics allows to deliver commodities just in time without losses

Rail

Ukraine has a vast network of railways connecting all agricultural areas with the maritime ports. Railways transportation is the cheapest way to deliver commodities and Ukraine has the cheapest railways rates in Europe
